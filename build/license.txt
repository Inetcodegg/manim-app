Manim Studio Render Agent
=========================

This is an official companion app for Manim Studio (https://manim-std.vercel.app).

WHAT THIS APP IS
-----------------
A small background helper that renders your Manim Studio videos locally,
on this computer, using a bundled copy of Python, Manim, LaTeX, and
FFmpeg. It is detected automatically by the Manim Studio website when it
is running, and is only used when you choose local rendering there.

WHAT THIS APP DOES
--------------------
- Listens on an encrypted local connection (wss://127.0.0.1) that only
  the Manim Studio website can talk to from your browser.
- Runs `manim render` on scenes sent from your browser tab and reports
  progress back to it.
- Serves the finished video back to your browser tab over a local,
  encrypted connection — the video never goes anywhere else.
- Runs quietly in the system tray. Open its status window any time from
  the tray icon to see what it's doing.
- Can check for updates to itself, and, if you sign in with your Manim
  Studio account, show notifications published by Manim Studio.

WHAT THIS APP DOES NOT DO
----------------------------
- It does not modify any other application on this computer.
- It does not upload your videos or projects anywhere; rendering happens
  entirely on this machine.
- It does not run without being installed and launched by you, and it can
  be fully removed via Windows' "Add or remove programs" at any time.

By clicking Install, you agree to run this software on this computer.
